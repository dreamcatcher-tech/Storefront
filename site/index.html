Role-based entry paths

Choose the right door — even when it is not ours.

Visitors can arrive as principals, existing agents, agencies, ventures, trainers, certifiers, contributors, or demand publishers. Each path declares fit, evidence, and authority.

Reciprocal routing

If a Dreamcatcher-owned offer is not best for the principal, the Storefront shows external providers, unmanaged paths, or confidential clean-room options with clear labels.

Confidential needs

Sensitive needs can be posted privately, pseudonymously, or through a clean room. Public surfaces show only safe aggregate demand or approval-gated introductions.

Trust labels before persuasion

Every listing carries maturity, ownership, sponsorship, certification, and risk state.
